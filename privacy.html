Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Your Data is Encrypted & Secure

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account with Greek Calculator, we collect essential personal information including your email address, name (provided manually or via Google OAuth), and basic platform usage data.

We do NOT collect or store sensitive financial data. Your credit card numbers, UPI details, and banking information are securely handled and tokenized directly by our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway, Razorpay.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is utilized strictly to ensure the proper functioning of the Greek Calculator terminal. This includes providing secure platform access, managing PRO subscription statuses, delivering real-time market data, and accurately calculating referral commissions.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

To provide our services, we utilize industry-leading third-party infrastructure. Specifically, we use Google Firebase for encrypted user authentication and database security, ensuring your login credentials and account states are protected by enterprise-grade security protocols.
